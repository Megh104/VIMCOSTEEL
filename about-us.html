About us


our company

VIMCO STEEL INDUSTRIES takes immense pleasure in introducing itself as one of the leading stockists and suppliers of high-quality Fittings, Pipes, and Tubes in various materials to meet the ultimate requirements of our customers.

Having established ourselves in the heart of Mumbai, we maintain adequate stock levels of all essential items within our supply scope and are well-positioned to offer immediate deliveries. In the event of non-availability of any specialized items, we are confident in our ability to supply the same at short notice, backed by a team of dynamic young professionals.

At VIMCO STEEL INDUSTRIES, we have been significantly involved in the major supply of Tube and Pipe Fittings in India. We are a client-focused and service-oriented company. The foundations of our business are built on quality, reputation, customer service, and performance. We place the highest emphasis on human resources and the deployment of the latest information technology.

VIMCO STEEL INDUSTRIES supplies Tube and Pipe fittings in various sizes, ranges, types, and schedule classes. We regularly supply our materials to core industries such as refineries, chemical and petrochemical plants, oil and gas sectors, water treatment plants, power plants, offshore facilities, shipbuilding, EPC, and other engineering companies.

Our corporate philosophy of “source, supply, service” is wholeheartedly embraced and practiced not only by the management but also by all staff members.
